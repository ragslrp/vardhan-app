Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 01, 2025

1. No Personal Data Collection

Vardhan Apps respects your privacy and does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users of our mobile games "Numbee - Arrange Numbers" and "Magic Square" or visitors to our website.

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. No Device Information Collection

We do not collect any device information such as operating system details, screen resolution, device type, or hardware specifications.

Our games and website function without requiring access to your device information.

3. No Game Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or analyze gameplay data, scores, statistics, or user behavior patterns.

Our games are designed to work locally on your device without sending data to our servers.

4. No Cookies or Tracking

Our website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of web tracking technology.

We do not monitor or analyze website usage patterns or visitor behavior.

5. No Third-Party Data Sharing

Since we collect no personal data, we have no information to share with third parties.

We do not integrate with analytics services, advertising networks, or social media platforms for data collection purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

Our mobile games are designed for general audiences and do not collect any personal information from users of any age.

Parents and guardians can be assured that no data is collected from children using our games.
